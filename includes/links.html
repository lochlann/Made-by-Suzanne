La Maison des Amis is a great place to simply relax - with the garden, pool, tennis, ping-pong, trampoline etc. But there's no shortage of things to see and do in the area. Here's the list of our favourites:

Tarn Tourism

The first place to look for more information on what to do and see in the region, including where to eat and what's on.

Les Jardins des Martels

Literally across the road, the second most visited paying tourist site in the Tarn. Stunning!

Tourist Train

For the wee ones, take the train that runs past the end of the garden from Les Jardins des Martels to St Lieux.

Cap Decouverte

Inexpensive and great fun park, transformed from an old open cast mine. Can be very hot in high summer.

Cri de Tarzan

This really is fun for all the family. If you've never tried it, it's a must! Great spot too for a picnic for those that prefer not to partake.

Canoeing

We are surrounded by rivers but its worth taking the 50 min drive to St Antoine Noble Val. Take a picnic and make a day of it!
