With 5 acres and a large pool, La Maison des Amis is a great place to relax. But the Tarn really is one of France's hidden treasures - there's no shortage of things to see and do in the area.

Here's a list of some of our favourites.

Tarn Tourism

The first place to look for more information on what to do and see in the region, including where to eat and what's on.

Les Jardins des Martels

Literally across the road, the second most visited paying tourist site in the Tarn. Wonderful!

Cri de Tarzan

This really is fun for all the family. If you've never tried it, it's a must! Great spot too for a picnic for those that prefer not to partake.

Canoeing

We are surrounded by rivers but its worth taking the 50 min drive to St Antoine Noble Val. Take a picnic and make a day of it!

Local restaurants

Le Colvert is a magical spot, particularly in summer, and just down the road. But it gets a real run for its money in L'Assiette au Comptoir. Honourable mentions too for Les Rosiers de Cocagne, L'Echauguette and La Pitcholina.

Local markets

A trip to the Tarn is not complete without a visit to one of the local markets.

Vineyards

A visit to France is not complete without a visit to the vineyards. La fête du vin de Gaillac is fabulous. The Friday night apéro concerts are great fun too!

Albi Cathedral

A must visit, the largest brick building in the world, it is stunning! And right beside - The Toulouse-Lautrec museum. Not to mention some very nice restaurants for lunch or dinner.

Airbus Factory

Calecio Spa

We don't go often enough but this place is great. Facilities are top class, you might even get to see the Stade Toulousain team!

Tourist Train

For the wee ones, take the train that runs past the end of the garden from Les Jardins des Martels to St Lieux.

Cap Decouverte

Inexpensive and great fun park, transformed from an old open cast mine. Can be very hot in high summer.

Paintball & Laserquest

Our kids love it! Paintball at Puylarens, Laserquest at Toulouse.

Stade Toulousain

Heineken Cup rugby against Toulouse - a great weekend!

Castres Olympic

Top 14 Champions 2013, a hard place to get a result, always a great atmosphere!

Quad Biking

The hills around Puylaurens are spectacular, you can even take the kids.

Even if you wanted, we suspect you would struggle to squeeze all that into a 2 week stay!
