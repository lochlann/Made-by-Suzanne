About me

I am Susan, “Welcome to my web site.

Where my aim is to make beautiful furniture pieces in wood that will last forever whether they be practical or just to be admired.

I am a graduate of The Chippendale International School of Furniture and also of the Institute of Professional Auctioneers and Valuers (Fine & Decorative Arts - architecture, furniture, art, silverware, rugs, costume, and decorations). In addition, my practical and artistic talents have been honed through my stucco work on restoring and reinstating ornate plasterwork.

In all cases, whether it be making or restoring, I endeavour to use traditional methods and materials wherever possible.

As every thing is handmade I feel it is a strength of my service to be able to provide exactly what my customer wants. So for example, if you need a non-standard size period-table to fit an alcove, and in a specific type of wood, then we can work together to achieve that.
